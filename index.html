CONCEPT & THOUGHT

Why ScholarsBangladesh.com?

Because it gives scholars from Bangladesh, whether they live in Bangladesh or overseas, a great networking opportunity. Right now, these scholars do not have a common network. Hundreds of fine scholars, working in fields as diverse as nuclear technology to medicine to media, are working all over the world. Scholarsbangladesh.com will help them come together under one common umbrella. With one touch on their keyboard, they can now communicate with each other, share their thoughts, and seek help in any personal or professional areas.
